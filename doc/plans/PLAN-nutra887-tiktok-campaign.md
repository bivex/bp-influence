# Nutra 887 TikTok Campaign Strategy

**Campaign Name**: Nutra 887 Launch - "Wellness Your Way"
**Date**: February 2025
**Status**: Draft Strategy
**Platform**: TikTok (primary) + Instagram Reels (amplification)

---

## Executive Summary

This document outlines a comprehensive TikTok influencer campaign strategy for the launch of Nutra 887, a new wellness/nutrition supplement. The campaign leverages TikTok's native content formats and algorithm to drive awareness, consideration, and conversion through authentic creator partnerships.

**Campaign Hypothesis**: TikTok's wellness community is highly engaged with transparent, science-backed product education from trusted creators. A strategy combining educational content with authentic lifestyle integration will outperform traditional advertising.

---

## 3. Platform Strategy (TikTok-Specific)

### Why TikTok for Nutra 887

| Factor | Rationale |
|--------|-----------|
| **Audience match** | 63% of TikTok users research wellness products on platform |
| **Content fit** | Educational content performs well in wellness niche |
| **Algorithm advantage** | Niche content can outperform larger accounts |
| **Authenticity premium** | Native, unpolished content outperforms ads |
| **Creator ecosystem** | Strong wellness creator community with high trust |

### Native TikTok Content Strategy

**Format Mix**:
- 60% Native educational (talking head, green screen, Duet)
- 25% Lifestyle integration (routine, day in life)
- 15% Trend participation with brand twist

**TikTok Algorithm Optimization**:

| Signal | Strategy |
|--------|----------|
| **Completion rate** | Aim for 15-30s videos; front-load hook |
| **Re-watch value** | Include educational "secrets" or transformations |
| **Shares** | Create shareable "did you know" moments |
| **Saves** | Make content reference-worthy (routines, guides) |
| **Comments** | Ask questions, prompt debate (e.g., "hot take") |
| **Sound** | Use trending sounds; create branded sound |

### Content Verticals to Target

1. **#WellnessEducation** - Science-backed ingredient breakdowns
2. **#RoutineTok** - Morning/night routine integrations
3. **#Biohacking** - Performance optimization focus
4. **#TransparentTok** - Behind the scenes, "nothing to hide"
5. **#RealWellness** - Anti-perfection, honest wellness

### Hashtag Strategy

**Campaign Hashtag**: #Nutra887Way

**Supporting Hashtags** (per post, mix 3-5):
- Category: #wellnesstok #supplementreview #ingredienteducation
- Vertical: #morningroutine #wellnesshacks #b2bwellness
- Campaign-specific: #Nutra887Way

**Trend Hashtags**: Use trending sounds/tags when on-brand (1 per video max)

## 4. Creator Mix & Tier Strategy

### Recommended Creator Pyramid

```
              1 Mega (Hero)
          3-4 Macro (Anchors)
        8-10 Mid-Tier (Core)
      20-25 Micro (Specialists)
    30-40 Nano (Authenticity Army)
```

### Detailed Tier Breakdown

#### Mega (1 creator): 1M+ followers

| Role | Creator Profile | Rationale |
|------|-----------------|-----------|
| **Hero Launch** | Celebrity wellness advocate, trusted voice | Mass awareness, PR value, establishes credibility |

**Deliverables**: 2 videos, 1 LIVE, cross-channel amplification

---

#### Macro (3-4 creators): 500K-1M followers

| Role | Creator Profile | Count |
|------|-----------------|-------|
| **Science/Expert Voice** | Nutritionist, doctor, or PhD in wellness space | 1-2 |
| **Lifestyle Authority** | Wellness lifestyle creator with aspirational aesthetic | 1-2 |

**Deliverables**: 2 videos each, IG Reel cross-post

---

#### Mid-Tier (8-10 creators): 50K-500K followers

| Vertical | Creator Profile | Count |
|----------|-----------------|-------|
| **Fitness + Wellness** | Trainers who preach holistic health | 3 |
| **Nutrition/Education** | Recipe developers, ingredient educators | 2-3 |
| **Lifestyle/Routine** | Day-in-life, routine creators | 2-3 |
| **Biohacking/Performance** | Data-driven wellness creators | 2 |

**Deliverables**: 2-3 videos each, Stories, comment engagement

---

#### Micro (20-25 creators): 10K-50K followers

| Vertical | Focus |
|----------|--------|
| **Niche Wellness** | Specific wellness areas (sleep, energy, focus) |
| **Local/Regional** | Geo-targeted creators for retail support |
| **Professional/Career** | Wellness for busy professionals |
| **Postpartum/Mom Wellness** | If product fits this audience |

**Deliverables**: 1-2 videos each, high engagement in comments

---

#### Nano (30-40 creators): 1K-10K followers

**Strategy**: "Authenticity Army" - everyday wellness enthusiasts

| Use Case | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **UGC Library** | Build content for paid amplification |
| **Community Seeding** | Comment on other posts, build grassroots buzz |
| **Niche Sub-communities** | Hyper-targeted wellness micro-communities |

**Deliverables**: 1 video, UGC rights, cross-commenting

---

### Quantity vs. Reach Trade-offs

| Approach | Pros | Cons |
|----------|------|------|
| **Fewer, Larger** | Bigger reach per creator, easier management | Lower engagement, less authentic, higher cost |
| **More, Smaller** | Higher engagement, authentic, resilient | More management, lower individual reach |
| **Pyramid Mix (Recommended)** | Balanced: reach + authenticity + efficiency | Complex coordination |

**Our Recommendation**: Pyramid mix - balances mass awareness with authentic engagement and provides content library for paid amplification.

---

### Creator Selection Criteria

**Must-Have Attributes**:
- Authentic wellness focus (not just sponsored content)
- Engagement rate above platform benchmarks
- Transparent audience demographics
- No recent competitor partnerships (60-day window)
- Professional communication and deliverable track record

**Exclusions** (Brand Safety):
- Promotes extreme diets or unhealthy body standards
- Has made controversial health claims
- Negative sentiment in recent content
- Bot/follower irregularities (checked via vetting)

### Posting Cadence

**Launch Phase (Weeks 1-2)**:
- Mega/Macro: 2 posts each, staggered Day 1 and Day 7
- Mid-tier: Distributed across launch window
- Micro/Nano: Ongoing throughout 2 weeks

**Sustain Phase (Weeks 3-8)**:
- Mid-tier: 1 additional post each
- Micro/Nano: Ongoing content
- Trend participation when relevant

**Live Content**:
- Mega/Macro: 1 LIVE each (Q&A format)
- Mid-tier: Optional LIVE for those with strong community

---

### Caption Strategy

**Structure**:
1. Hook (first line): Attention-grabbing claim or question
2. Value: What viewer will learn
3. Personal touch: Creator's genuine experience
4. Disclosure: Clear #ad disclosure
5. CTA: Link in bio + specific next step
6. Hashtags: 3-5 relevant, 1 campaign-specific

**Example Template**:
```
[HOOK] The supplement industry doesn't want you to read this label...

[VALUE] Breaking down exactly what's in Nutra 887 and why it's different from everything else I've tried.

[PERSONAL] I've been taking it for 30 days and here's my honest experience...

[DISCLOSURE] #ad Paid partnership with @nutra887

[CTA] Link in bio to shop + use code [CODE] for 15% off

[HASHTAGS] #wellnesstok #ingredienteducation #Nutra887Way
```

---

### Visual Direction

**Tone**: Native, authentic, non-polished

| DO | DON'T |
|----|-------|
| Natural lighting, home settings | Over-produced studio looks |
| Creator's authentic style | Forced brand aesthetics |
| Product in real-use context | Staged, posed product shots |
| Behind-the-scenes moments | Stock footage or obvious ads |
| Creative transitions | Generic TikTok trends without brand tie-in |

**Product Visibility**:
- Hero shot in first 3 seconds OR reveal format (build to product)
- Natural integration (on counter, in hand, in routine)
- Close-up shots of product/texture when relevant
- Lifestyle shots over isolated product shots

## 10. Legal & Compliance

### Disclosure Requirements

| Platform | Requirement |
|----------|-------------|
| TikTok | Native "Paid Partnership" toggle + #ad |
| Instagram (cross-posts) | Paid Partnership tag + #ad |
| FTC Compliance | Clear, conspicuous disclosure at start |

**Key Requirements**:
- "Paid Partnership" toggle activated on all posts
- #ad or #sponsored in first 3 lines of caption
- No misleading health claims (supplement category)
- Clear distinction between paid and organic content

---

### Usage Rights

| Right Type | Duration | Scope |
|------------|----------|-------|
| **Paid Use** | 12 months | TikTok/IG amplification, Spark Ads |
| **UGC License** | Perpetual | Internal use, case studies, portfolio |
| **Exclusivity** | 60 days | No competitor supplement partnerships |

---

### Key Contract Clauses

1. **Deliverable Specifications**: Exact deliverables, timing, quality standards
2. **Compliance**: FTC, TikTok, supplement category regulations
3. **Approval Process**: Pre-approval rights, response timelines
4. **Cancellation**: Termination for cause, payment structure
5. **Morals Clause**: Brand safety protections
6. **Governing Law**: Jurisdiction for disputes

---

### Supplement-Specific Compliance

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Health Claims** | No disease treatment claims; structure/function only |
| **Testimonials** | Clear "your results may vary"; no guaranteed outcomes |
| **Before/After** | Not recommended unless substantiated |
| **Expert Endorsements** | Must disclose credentials if presented as expert |
| **FDA Compliance** | No unsubstantiated claims; appropriate disclaimers |
