### 9. Recovery & De-escalation

**Short-term (1-7 days)**:
- [ ] Continue proactive updates (every ___ hours while active)
- [ ] Track coverage narrative shift
- [ ] Thank supportive voices
- [ ] Begin corrective actions publicly

**Medium-term (1-4 weeks)**:
- [ ] Publish follow-up statement with results of investigation
- [ ] Launch corrective initiative / campaign
- [ ] Resume normal content and campaigns (when appropriate)

**Long-term (1-6 months)**:
- [ ] Conduct full post-crisis review
- [ ] Update crisis playbook with learnings
- [ ] Rebuild reputation through positive storytelling
- [ ] Monitor long-tail sentiment recovery
