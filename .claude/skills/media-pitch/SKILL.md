### 1. Standard Email Pitch

**Subject line options** (pick the strongest):
- A: [Specific + newsworthy, under 50 characters]
- B: [Data-driven angle]
- C: [Question that creates curiosity]

---

Hi [Journalist first name],

[1 sentence — personal connection: reference their recent article, beat, or interest. Show you've done homework.]

### 2. Exclusive Offer Pitch

**Subject**: Exclusive: [headline-worthy summary]

---

Hi [Name],

I'm reaching out with an exclusive opportunity for [Outlet] — [1 sentence describing the exclusive].

**The story**: [2-3 sentences — what the news is, why it matters, key data point]

**Why [Outlet]**: [1 sentence — why this fits their audience specifically]

**What we can offer**:
- Exclusive first look / data / interview
- Embargo until: [date/time]
- [Executive name] available for interview
- High-res assets provided

If you're interested, I can send over the full details and embargo terms. Would [date] work for a brief call?

Best,
[Your name]

## Pitch Personalisation Checklist

Before sending any pitch, verify:

- [ ] **Journalist name** spelled correctly
- [ ] **Outlet name** correct (not a competitor outlet!)
- [ ] **Referenced their work** — specific article, not generic
- [ ] **Beat match** — this story fits what they actually cover
- [ ] **Timing** — not pitching during a major breaking news day
- [ ] **Subject line** — under 50 chars, no clickbait, clear value
- [ ] **Attachments** — NO attachments in cold pitches (link to assets instead)
- [ ] **Ask** — clear and specific (interview, exclusive, review, comment)
- [ ] **Length** — entire pitch visible without scrolling (under 200 words)
- [ ] **Signature** — includes phone number for easy callback
