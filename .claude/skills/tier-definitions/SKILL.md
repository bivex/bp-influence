### Strategic Tier Mix Recommendations

#### By Campaign Objective

| Objective | Recommended mix | Rationale |
|-----------|----------------|-----------|
| Awareness (mass reach) | 20% Macro/Mega + 30% Mid + 50% Micro | Hero reach + community spread |
| Consideration / Education | 10% Mid + 50% Micro + 40% Nano | Trust + depth of content |
| Conversion / Sales | 20% Mid + 50% Micro + 30% Nano | Engagement + authenticity drives action |
| Brand launch | 10% Mega + 30% Macro + 40% Mid + 20% Micro | Splash + sustained visibility |
| Always-on / Community | 30% Micro + 70% Nano | Constant authentic presence |
| UGC / Content production | 20% Micro + 80% Nano | Volume + authenticity |

#### By Budget Level

| Budget | Recommended approach |
|--------|---------------------|
| $1K-5K | 5-15 Nano creators |
| $5K-20K | 3-5 Micro + 10-15 Nano |
| $20K-50K | 2-3 Mid + 5-10 Micro + Nano seeding |
| $50K-150K | 1 Macro + 3-5 Mid + 10 Micro + Nano army |
| $150K-500K | 1-2 Mega/Macro + Mid-tier programme + Micro roster |
| $500K+ | Full pyramid: Mega hero + Macro + Mid + Micro + Nano |

### Tier Transition: What to Watch

Creators often move between tiers. Key signals:

| Signal | Meaning | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Rapid follower growth + stable engagement | Star on the rise | Lock in NOW at current rates |
| Follower growth + dropping engagement | Possible bought followers or virality wearing off | Monitor, don't increase spend |
| Stable followers + rising engagement | Deepening audience loyalty | Great long-term partner |
| Declining followers + declining engagement | Fading relevance | Phase out or renegotiate |
| Cross-platform expansion | Growing influence | Multi-platform deal opportunity |
