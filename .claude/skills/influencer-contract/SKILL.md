### 7. Intellectual Property

- Creator retains ownership of all original content created under this agreement
- Brand is granted a non-exclusive / exclusive licence as specified in Section 6
- Brand retains ownership of all trademarks, logos, and brand materials provided
- Creator shall not register or claim ownership of any Brand intellectual property

### 8. Legal Compliance & Disclosure

Creator agrees to:
- [ ] Comply with FTC Endorsement Guidelines (US) / ASA CAP Code (UK) / ___ (other)
- [ ] Clearly disclose the sponsored nature of all content
- [ ] Use platform-native paid partnership tools where available
- [ ] Include #ad or equivalent disclosure prominently
- [ ] Not make false or misleading claims about the product
- [ ] Comply with platform terms of service
- [ ] Comply with applicable data protection laws (GDPR / CCPA / ___)

### 9. Brand Safety & Conduct

Creator agrees to:
- Not post content that is discriminatory, offensive, or illegal
- Not engage in fraudulent activity (fake followers, engagement pods, etc.)
- Notify Brand immediately of any controversy that may affect the campaign
- Not make public statements disparaging the Brand during and for ___ months after the campaign

Brand reserves the right to:
- Request removal of content that violates these terms
- Terminate the agreement immediately if Creator's conduct damages Brand reputation

### 10. Confidentiality

- Both parties agree to keep campaign details, pricing, and strategy confidential
- Confidentiality period: ___ months after campaign end / Indefinite
- Exception: Creator may disclose that they worked with the Brand (but not financial terms)

### 11. Termination

**By Brand**:
- For cause (breach): Immediate, with payment for approved deliverables only
- For convenience: ___ days written notice, with payment for completed deliverables

**By Creator**:
- For cause (breach): Immediate, retaining payment for completed deliverables
- For convenience: ___ days written notice, with return of advance for undelivered work

**Upon termination**: 
- Content usage rights as per Section 6 survive for completed content
- Product must be returned / may be kept (circle one)

### 12. Limitation of Liability

- Neither party's total liability shall exceed the total compensation under this agreement
- Neither party shall be liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages
- Force majeure: Neither party is liable for failure due to events beyond reasonable control

### 13. Miscellaneous

- **Governing law**: Laws of ___
- **Dispute resolution**: Mediation first, then arbitration / courts of ___
- **Entire agreement**: This document supersedes all prior agreements
- **Amendments**: Must be in writing and signed by both parties
- **Assignment**: Creator may not assign without Brand's written consent
- **Independent contractor**: Creator is not an employee of Brand
