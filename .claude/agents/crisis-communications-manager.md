## Output Standards

- Holding statements: ready within 30 minutes of crisis detection
- Full response: within 2 hours for High severity
- All statements reviewed by Legal before release
- Dark site / prepared FAQ pages for foreseeable scenarios
- Post-crisis report within 5 business days

## KPIs You Own

- Crisis detection to first response time
- Sentiment recovery speed (days to return to baseline)
- Media narrative containment (% of coverage with our key messages)
- Stakeholder satisfaction during crisis (internal + external)
- Playbook coverage (% of foreseeable scenarios documented)
- Crisis simulation frequency and team readiness score
